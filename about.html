About soyun lee

From a typical high school student to an atypical explorer

All humans are said to be unique: some are hot-tempered but also considerate, and some are calm but passionate; I cannot list all the personalities of 7.9 billion people.

We are still very similar to human beings, though.
No individual can live solely on their own, and we have basic needs to belong.
Therefore, since my upbringing, I have strongly believed that humans are unique only in the combinations of traits that each of us is made up of.

For example, I am a person who consists of compassion, eccentricity, and eagerness for exploration.
Since love is what I was consistently encouraged with, I want to encourage all people around me with my love.
While I am a very compassionate individual, I often define myself as an eccentric writer, where I try to create stories that have never been told in the world in my own language.
Moreover, my eagerness for exploration has led me to a larger world of STEM which enabled me to expand my interests in neuroscience.

By identifying my traits and how they affect me, I was able to overcome the challenges that were upon me by reinforcing to myself, who am I?
