From a Dreamer to a Writer

The very reason I had started writing was that I wanted to create a world where my dreams could come true.
of My dreams have always been peculiar since I was young. While others dreamt of their future of being a doctor, ballerina, or a teacher, my dream was to move to an alternate world surrounded by non-human creatures.
When I had a chance to give a speech about this dream, while the teachers had told me to dream something else, my mom was the one who encouraged me to write those ideas so that I don’t forget them.
I started to write short stories that reflected my dreams.
Sometimes surreal, sometimes dreamlike. creating a world with my rules was not the best part of all, however.

The ultimate best part was the characters.
Creating identities that don’t exist in real life - a sense of being a little god with a small world.
