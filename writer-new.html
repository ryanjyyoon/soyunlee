JOTTING DOWN MEMORIES

From my memories to imagination, from my thoughts to concrete stories

From a Storyteller to a Writer

I used to dream about many things: Why would someone act like that? What goes on in the universe? What's going on in my friend's mind?
Gathering knowledge from these thoughts, I became fond of the thoughts that came to my mind.
The stories I thought of turned into written diaries and seasoned with more imagination became books.
